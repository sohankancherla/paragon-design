# Cookie Policy

_Last updated: August 2025_

This Cookie Policy explains how Paragon ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and platform. By using Paragon, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us operate our platform, provide essential features, and improve your experience.

## How We Use Cookies

Paragon uses cookies for the following purposes:

- **Authentication:** To securely log you in and maintain your session while using our platform.
- **Analytics:** We use PostHog to collect information about how you interact with our platform. This helps us analyze usage and improve our services.
- **Payments:** Stripe may set cookies to process payments and prevent fraud.

## Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services integrated into Paragon. These include:

- **PostHog** (analytics)
- **Stripe** (payments)
- **Google** (for Google Calendar integration; may set cookies as part of authentication)

We do not control the cookies set by third parties. Please refer to their respective privacy and cookie policies for more information.

## Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can remove or block cookies at any time, but some features of Paragon may not function properly without them.

## Cookie Consent

We do not use a cookie consent banner. Cookies are set as required for platform functionality and third-party integrations.
